Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Here are a few of the questions that we get a lot. Of course, we're happy to answer any question you may have!

Is Spark open source?
Yes, Spark is 100% open source. If you feel up to it, you can go through our simple and well documented codebase to see how things work! We also appreciate any contributions to the project - so check us out on GitHub!
How do I know if my funds are safe?
Your private keys never need to be entered into Spark. That makes using Spark pretty safe. Nobody will have access to any of the funds you receive at any time.
Does Spark do fiat conversion?
Yes. Technically. Spark supports static addresses so that merchants can convert to the currency of their choice instantly using uphold.com - we do not charge a fee for this, but uphold charges ~1.25%
Does Spark charge any fees?
No. Spark is completely free to use.
